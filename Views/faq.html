Frequently asked questions

Is MoBPS free to use?

MoBPS is available under GNU 3.0 License. This means its free to use for both academia and commercial use!

And your software simulate my breeding program
I am assuming yes. But it helps if you tell me what you want to simulate =)
Usually its best to first think about the key steps of your simulation (drawing usually helps) and factors you want to include.
If you think your simulation is not possible, asking us if it is actually possible or if we can add new features usually helps.

How to learn more about MoBPS?

Extended documentation on the R-package can be found at Github.
Introduction for the interface in form of slides and videos can be found at here .
Due to the current CoVid-19 situation no workshops on the use of MoBPS are scheduled.

What happens to MoBPS after Torstens PostDoc?

Our Team that is working at and with MoBPS growing with some positions with contracts till 2023.
In any case, constantly growing the framework is intended to be a big building block of the work group. Therefore continues support should be assured.

My simulations do not finish?!

Resources per user in our cluster are limited as the backend server has just 20 cores and 64 GB memory.
Either export your JSON files to R and run the simulations on your own system or ask us help you set up your own MoBPS server.
